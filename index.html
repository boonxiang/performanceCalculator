Why the dashboard does not show full?

Performance Dashboard

The dashboard not show the full performance data due to the conversion DC to AC have losses, and the inverter may not be able to convert all the DC energy produced by the solar panels into AC energy and will not more than 80%. This can result in a lower-than-expected performance reading on the dashboard. Additionally, the performance data will be updated in real-time every five minutes.

What is Energy Clipping?

Energy clipping happens when solar electric (photovoltaic) panels provide more power than an inverter can handle. Clipping is lost power production
